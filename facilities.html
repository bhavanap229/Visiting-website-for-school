Our Facilities

Space and Size of Classrooms

Overcrowded classrooms often lead to reduced student engagement and improper learning. Teachers also find it difficult to manage an overcrowded class. Every student is not able to get the required attention. So, every classroom should have moderate number of students. Additionally, it should be spacious enough to offer adequate and personal space for the students.

Computer Laboratory

With these factors, parents and schools must ensure that they provide the aforementioned list of basic school facilities to students for their well-being along with quality education.Computer education is essential for today’s generation, so it should be available to all the students.

Installation of CCTV

Everywhere in school, each classroom, corridor, corner of school we have CCTV cameras. The CCTV cameras always be on and be continuously monitored. So that anything happens in a school that can be seen by anyone. Placed cameras at noticeable places throughout the school take advantage of school safety.

Purified Drinking Water Facilities

The school has the facility of water filter & cooler to provide the students with pure, hygenic & cooled drinking water.

Medical Centre

The school has a well-equipped health unit, manned by a full time doctor and a qualified nurse. We strive to provide our students a safe and secure environment, free from any health hazards. In the case of accidents or emergencies, the medical centre is well equipped to provide sufficient first aid.

Well-Maintained Toilets

Adequate toilet facilities assume great importance in the school premises and they need to be constructed as per the number of students enrolled. In the 21st century, many schools still don’t have an adequate infrastructure that hinders the overall growth of the students. Regular cleaning and washing of the toilets is essential to avoid bacteria and infections.
