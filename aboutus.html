About Us

Welcome to Dnyan Mandir Primary School !

Dnyan Mandir Marathi-English Primary School was established in 2006 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Urban area of SURAT CORPO. block of SURAT district of Gujrat. The school consists of Grades from Nursery to 10. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section.
We are here for you from 15+ years and giving our best results!
Since opening our students examination results have risen to their highest ever levels and our school enjoys an excellent reputation for exemplary behaviour and outstanding student leadership. We combine traditional values (We expect every child to "Learn, Engage & Respect") with cutting edge technology.

MISSION

We are committed to providing a positive learning environment that advocates considerate, responsible, and emotionally healthy behaviors. Each individual student will feel a part of the Central community, which promotes positive behavioral development.

VISSION

To educate all students to the highest levels of academic achievement, to enable them to reach and expand their potential, and to prepare them to become productive, responsible, ethical, creative and compassionate members of society!

GOALS
